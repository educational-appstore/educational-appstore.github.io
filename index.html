Sawfish App Store

A new way for students to access fun, safe, and useful apps at school.

Why We Built This

Many schools rely on tools like Self Service, but students often find it limited, slow, or missing the apps they actually want to use.

A group of student developers created the Sawfish App Store — a clean, fast, modern web app that feels like a real App Store, built specifically for school-safe fun and communication tools.

How to Install This App

The Sawfish App Store works best when it is added to your iPad’s Home Screen. Follow the steps below to complete setup:

  1. Tap the Share button at the top-right of Safari.
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the top-right corner.
  4. Return to your Home Screen and open the Sawfish App Store from its icon.

What You Get After Installation

Once the app is installed, you’ll unlock access to:

  • The Sawfish Game Hub — a curated set of quick-play, school-friendly games.
  • Chat Portal — your link to the student chat app.
  • Call App Launcher — start school-safe calls instantly.
  • Automatic full-screen mode — feels like a native iPad app.
  • Clean iOS-style navigation with smooth transitions.

After installation, this screen disappears and the full app interface becomes available.
